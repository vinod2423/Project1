VISION AND MISSION


VISION OF DEPARTMENT


Our vision is to emerge as a world class Computer Science and
Engineering department through excellent teaching and strong research
environment that responds swiftly to the challenges of changing computer
science technology and addresses technological needs of the
stakeholders.


MISSION OF DEPARTMENT


To enable our students to master the fundamental principles of computing
and to develop in them the skills needed to solve practical problems using
contemporary computer-based technologies and practices to cultivate a
community of professionals who will serve the public as resources on
state-of-the-art computing science and information technology.
